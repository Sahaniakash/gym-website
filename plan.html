Basic Membership:
Access: Gym floor access only, during standard hours. Features: Access to cardio and strength equipment. Cost: Monthly and annual pricing. Benefits: Ideal for members who want to work out independently.

Standard Membership:
Access: Includes gym floor and group fitness classes. Features: Access to all workout equipment, and a range of fitness classes like yoga, spinning, and HIIT. Cost: Monthly and annual pricing with discounts for yearly sign-ups. Benefits: Great for those who want the flexibility of classes along with gym access.

Premium Membership:
Access: All Standard Membership features, plus personal training sessions, exclusive areas (like saunas or pools), or extended hours. Features: Includes personal training consultations, nutrition guidance, and additional amenities. Cost: Premium pricing with savings for long-term commitments. Benefits: Designed for members seeking a full-service experience
